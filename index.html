What is PatriotHacks?

PatriotHacks is a collegiate hackathon held at George Mason University in Fairfax, Virginia. Join us for 48 hours of hacking in our all new makerspace, also known as the MIX (Mason Innovation Xchange)!

To learn more about the MIX, check out their website at https://www.mix.gmu.edu/

FAQ

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an invention marathon. Students come together to build cool software & hardware hacks over 24-48 hours.

How much does it cost?

Nothing. Attending a hackathon is free.

Do I need to be a student to attend?

Yes. Only students who are currently enrolled at a college/university or have graduated within the past 12 months are eligible to attend.

What if I have other questions?

If you have questions that are not answered here, feel free to send us an email at patriothacksgmu@gmail.com
